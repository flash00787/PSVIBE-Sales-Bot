## Overview
Split Fiction is the third major title from Hazelight Studios, following the acclaimed **A Way Out** (2018) and **It Takes Two** (2021) [^1].  
Revealed at The Game Awards 2024, it launched on March 6, 2025, and continues the studio’s commitment to mandatory two‑player cooperative play.  

The game’s central conceit is a clash of creative visions: Mio pens gritty sci‑fi narratives filled with neon megacities, hoverbikes, and AI overlords, while Zoe conjures high‑fantasy realms of enchanted forests, shapeshifting, and mythical beasts. When an experimental story‑simulation machine malfunctions, their worlds bleed together, and the two must navigate the resulting chaos while confronting the trauma that inspired their writing.
