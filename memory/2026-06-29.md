### Payment Method Parsing Bug (CRITICAL)
- **Bug:** `payment_method = 'Cash'` exact match never found because column stores `Cash:10000`, `KPay:31000|Cash:20000|WavePay:17000`
- **Fix:** Fetch all rows, parse in Python: split by `|` for multi-method, split by `:` to extract method+amount

### Cash Movement Integration
- Sources: `cash_movements` table (eject, transfer_out, transfer_in, inject)
- **transfer_out amounts are NEGATIVE** in DB — must use `ABS(amount)` in SQL queries
- **Double-negative bug:** `expected = ... - _tx_out` where _tx_out = -50,000 → became +50,000

### Expected Formula (Final)
```
Expected = Opening + Cash Sales + Transfer In + Inject − Eject − Transfer Out
```
- Eject: staff takes cash for stock/expenses (amount = POSITIVE in DB)
- Transfer Out: cash moved to KPay/Bank (amount = NEGATIVE in DB, use ABS)
- Transfer In: cash received from other accounts
- Inject: cash injected into drawer
