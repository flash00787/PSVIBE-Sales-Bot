### Food Commission тАФ Shop-Wide
- **Revenue** = `SUM(total) FROM stock_out` (actual items sold)
- **COGS** = `stock_fifo.calc_fifo()` (FIFO inventory cost)
- **Profit** = Revenue тИТ COGS
- **Commission** = staff_rate% ├Ч shop_profit (all staff use same shop profit)
- June example: rev=2,490,900, cogs=1,552,521, profit=938,379

### Game Bonus тАФ Shop-Wide Tiers
| Shop Play Hours | Bonus |
|----------------|-------|
| 1,500+ | 30,000 Ks |
| 1,800+ | 50,000 Ks |
| 2,000+ | 100,000 Ks |
Non-stacking, highest tier only. All staff get same bonus.
